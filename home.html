GMU SALSA CLUB


Azúcar, GMU Salsa Club

Azucar was mainly created to establish a Salsa dance community here at GMU and through that inspire others to join us. As well as to share the joys of salsa dancing with the GMU community through lessons and performances.

Our Mission

Azucar's mission is to share the joys of Salsa dancing with the George Mason University community and to create a fun social group that will foster a life long appreciation for dancing. Azucar offers an entirely fantastic way for people to come together and learn how to dance. We believe that enabling people to learn to dance, not only will fuel an un-extinguishable passion for it but will also connect the community we live in.Whether they are a beginner who believes they have two left feet or an expert who can dance blindfolded; Azucar gives people a fun and enriching way to join together and learn a new type of Salsa dancing (Rueda!).

Classes

Are you interested in learning how to dance?

Then join us as we kick off the spring semester with our first (Miami style) salsa lesson. Classes will take place at George Mason University at the Johnson Center (Dance studio). The Salsa classes will run every week until the end of the semester and It's completely FREE. We will have 2 hours of instructional beginners class from 7-9pm and a dance social from 9-11pm. No experience or partner needed. Also, Fridays we have tutoring sessions for people that want to practice or learn new types of dances; location and time will be announced earlier in the week. Check our Facebook group for class updates and class locations.
